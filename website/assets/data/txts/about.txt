EPS Welding Services is a custom metal welding &amp; fabrication service located in Los Angeles, CA.  Owned by Edward Sayadyan with over 30 years experience in precision metal welding, fabrication, repairs, and installation.  EPS Welding Services specializes in the fabrication of structural steel - beams, columns, trusses, bracing, bollards, lintels, etc, - as well as railings, stairs, securitys gates, doors, windows, ladders, and many more custom welding and fabrication projects.  We do same day custom welding, fabrication, repairs, and installation for residents residing near Los Angeles, San Fernando Valley, and surrounding major local cities.
